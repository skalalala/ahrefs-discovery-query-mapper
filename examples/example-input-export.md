## Product positioning brief

The Giving Block helps nonprofits accept cryptocurrency donations and reach crypto donors.

Audience:
- Nonprofit fundraising leaders
- Development teams at charities and universities
- Operations or finance stakeholders who care about donation processing, compliance, and donor experience

Positioning:
- The Giving Block helps nonprofits accept cryptocurrency donations and reach crypto donors.
- The product is for nonprofit fundraising, not retail payments or crypto trading.
- The platform helps nonprofits add crypto donation options without building crypto payment infrastructure themselves.
- Important themes: crypto donations, nonprofit fundraising, donor experience, year-end giving, tax-efficient giving, compliance support, and fundraising growth.

Differentiators:
- Built specifically for nonprofits
- Handles crypto donation processing
- Helps nonprofits reach donors who prefer to give crypto
- Supports fundraising campaigns and donor-facing donation pages

Avoid:
- Do not position the product as a crypto exchange.
- Do not position the product as general payment processing for businesses.
- Do not imply investment advice.

Competitors / alternatives:
- Every.org
- Donorbox
- Classy
- Coinbase Commerce
